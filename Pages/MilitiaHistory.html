Arkansas Militia History

The Arkansas Militia History exhibit explores the origins and evolution of Arkansas's military forces before the establishment of the modern National Guard. This exhibit showcases artifacts, documents, and stories from Arkansas's militia predecessors, highlighting their role in state defense and national conflicts.

Key Highlights

  • Early Militia Formation: Documents and artifacts from the territorial period and early statehood.
  • Civil War Era: Exhibits on Arkansas units that served in both Union and Confederate forces.
  • Post-Civil War Development: The transition to organized militia and the establishment of the Arkansas National Guard.
  • Historical Artifacts: Uniforms, weapons, and personal items from militia soldiers.

This exhibit demonstrates the long tradition of service that led to the modern Arkansas National Guard.
