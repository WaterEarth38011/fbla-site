Walk of Honor

The Walk of Honor is an outdoor memorial walkway featuring customizable bricks that honor veterans, events, and loved ones. This living memorial allows individuals and families to commemorate service and sacrifice.

About the Walk

  • Customizable Bricks: Personalized bricks honoring veterans, military units, or special events.
  • Memorial Purpose: A place for reflection and remembrance of Arkansas's military heritage.
  • Ordering Process: Bricks can be ordered through our partner at thatsmybrick.com/arngmuseum.
  • Community Involvement: Open to all who wish to honor military service, not limited to veterans.

The Walk of Honor serves as a lasting tribute to the brave men and women who have served Arkansas and our nation.
