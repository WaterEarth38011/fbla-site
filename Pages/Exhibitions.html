Exhibitions

Discover our permanent and special exhibitions that bring the history of the Arkansas National Guard to life.

  • World War I Gallery: Artifacts and stories from Arkansas Guardsmen in the Great War.
  • World War II Exhibit: Featuring the 153rd Infantry Regiment and their service overseas.
  • Cold War Era: Military equipment and uniforms from the post-WWII period.
  • Modern Operations: Displays on contemporary missions and deployments.

New exhibits are added regularly to honor our ongoing legacy.
