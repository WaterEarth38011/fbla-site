Yeater Collection

The Yeater Collection is a notable historical artifact collection that forms a cornerstone of the museum's holdings. This comprehensive collection showcases military memorabilia and artifacts that illustrate Arkansas's military heritage.

Collection Highlights

  • Military Memorabilia: A wide range of items from various eras of Arkansas military history.
  • Personal Artifacts: Items belonging to Arkansas soldiers and their families.
  • Historical Documents: Letters, diaries, and records from military service.
  • Equipment and Uniforms: Authentic military gear from different periods.

The Yeater Collection provides a tangible connection to Arkansas's rich military past.
